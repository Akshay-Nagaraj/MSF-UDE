\begin{abstract}
    We discuss a novel approach to jointly estimate the fitness of different variants and their effective reproduction numbers using a Multi-Strain Universal Differential Equation Model. 
    This method fits a single mechanistic multi-strain compartmental system in which time varying quantities are neural functions learned inside a stiff ODE solver preserving uncertainty propogation over separate analyses. 
\end{abstract}
